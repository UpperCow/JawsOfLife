What Has Media Done Wrong?

Movie Media Claim Fact or Theory
Jurassic Park You cannot be seen if you do not move. Fact: False. You might want to start running. T-Rex eyes are that of popular birds of prey. Also, its sense of smell is very potent.
Jurassic World Running away from a T-Rex. Theory: True. However, you can outWALK a T-Rex. It is said that T-Rex are incapable of running.
Jurassic Park T-Rex roar is very loud. Theory: False. Sadly, it is believed that T-Rex is very much in tune with that of a bird, and it has tonsils and lungs like that of a bird. Better start tweeting.
Almost every dinosaur movie prior to year 2001 All dinosaurs have scales. Theory: False. It is actually now believed that carnivorous dinosaurs actually have feathers.
Almost every dinosaur movie prior to year 2001 T-Rex arms are almost useless Fact: False. Though they are relatively small in comparison their large bodies, they can actually lift over four hundred pounds per arm.
